Jeans Whiskers

Whisker is a type of jeans design in which it uses design patterns taken from the worn out lines and impression patterns generated by natural wearing. Most of these patterns are imprinted on the hip, front thigh and back thigh area. These effect's purpose is to make the jeans look "vintage".

Whiskers are artificially created by hand sanding prewashed jeans(technical term: raw denim).

Although these designs and patterns are heavily demanded, the hand sanding process is time consuming as well as dangerous for worker's health. The process emits toxic dust which is potassium permaganate which is harmful when accidentally inhaled.

This is why factories are starting to use a laser machine which has a functionality to "hand sand" denim without workers getting exposure of the toxic dust. These machines lasers denim based on designs created in photoshop, this is where my role as a head graphic designer comes in, to design jeans whiskers that would be used to for imprinting whiskers on jeans.

My role

Design jeans whisker pattern that would be use in the laser machine
